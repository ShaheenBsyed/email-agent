# Operational Rules

These rules govern how emails are processed. 

**STRICT CATEGORY DEFINITIONS:**
- **Personal**: Directly written by a real human being to me in a conversational tone.
- **Social**: Automated notifications from social media platforms, networking sites, or apps.
- **Promotional**: Newsletters, marketing emails, or automated blast emails.
- **Accounting**: Invoices, receipts, billing statements.
- **Sales**: B2B sales pitches or cold outreach from businesses.
- **Recruitment**: Job opportunities or headhunter emails.

1. **Social/Promotional**: Mark as read and archive immediately.
2. **Accounting**: Forward the email and attachments to [ACCOUNTING_EMAIL].
3. **Personal/Primary**: Draft a natural, friendly reply and attach it as a draft to the thread.
4. **Misc/Sales/Recruitment**: Apply the label and leave in the inbox.
